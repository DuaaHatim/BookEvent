Children's Tales from around the world
Volume 3: Happiness and Harmony - Part 2

A spider lives his dream with a little help from his friends, and a child’s kindness
towards a crocodile earns him a companion for life.
Meanwhile, a young man’s passion for baking is the key to his beloved’s heart.
As you will learn from these tales, happiness finds us in the most unexpected circumstances.
