Children's Tales from around the world
Volume 3: Happiness and Harmony - Part 1

A woodcutter forms an unlikely friendship with a fearsome demon,
and a rainbow surprises newlyweds on a stormy day.
An old man gives up his worldly goods in the pursuit of happiness,
while two robots experience wonder when they discover their emotions.
